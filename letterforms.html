Letterforms

January 2023

Tools: Pen

As part of my Form and Color class, we were assigned to create a grid of designs using different letterforms. We started by using pen and paper to create 30 illustrations, utilizing the lines, curves, and shapes of the letters to produce visually captivating and interesting designs. We then narrowed down our selection to 8 designs to enlarge, and after that, we selected only 6 designs.
